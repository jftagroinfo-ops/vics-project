Global Spice Trade: Turmeric Market Outlook 2026

Market Report May 01, 2026 8 min read 4,200 views

As European demand for curcumin-rich Indian turmeric surges, importers are facing a tightening supply chain. Here is our 2026 outlook for Turmeric Finger and Powder exports.

Current Market Dynamics

The Nizamabad and Erode mandis have seen a 15% reduction in early arrivals this season. This supply crunch, coupled with rising pharmaceutical demand in the EU for high-curcumin (3-5%) turmeric, has pushed FOB prices up.

Price Projections

We anticipate prices to remain firm through Q3. Buyers are advised to lock in annual contracts now rather than purchasing on spot basis.
