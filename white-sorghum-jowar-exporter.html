Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?
Minimum order is 1 FCL (Full Container Load) — approximately 24–27 MT in a 20ft container or 26–28 MT in a 40ft container depending on the product. For samples, there is no minimum — we dispatch free samples globally.
What payment terms do you accept?
We accept LC at sight, TT (T/T), and DP. For established buyers, we offer open credit terms after initial orders. All prices are quoted CIF (Cost, Insurance, Freight) or FOB as per buyer preference.
What certifications and documents do you provide?
We provide: Certificate of Origin, Phytosanitary Certificate, Quality Analysis Certificate, Fumigation Certificate, Packing List, Commercial Invoice, and Bill of Lading. We are ISO 9001:2015 certified, APEDA registered, and a recognised Star Export House.
How long does shipping take?
Typical transit times: UAE/Gulf: 8–12 days | East Africa: 12–18 days | West Africa: 18–26 days | Europe: 22–30 days | SE Asia: 12–18 days. All from JNPT/Nhava Sheva, Mumbai or Mundra Port.
Can I get a free sample before placing an order?
Yes. We dispatch free samples worldwide — typically 250g–500g by courier. Request your free sample on our Sample Request page. Processing within 24 hours.
