What is CIF?

Cost + Insurance + Freight. JFT covers shipping to your port. You handle import customs.

What is FOB?

Free On Board. JFT loads goods onto the vessel. You arrange and pay for shipping & insurance.

What is Ex-Mill?

Factory gate price. You collect from our mill in India. Lowest cost, highest buyer responsibility.
