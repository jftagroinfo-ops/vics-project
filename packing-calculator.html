How It Works

How the calculator works

Based on real container specifications and product bulk densities used in Indian agro export practice.

1

Container dimensions

We use ISO standard internal dimensions for 20ft, 40ft, and 40ft HC containers — not nominal exterior sizes.

2

Bulk density lookup

Each product has a known bulk density (kg/CBM). Rice ≈ 780, cumin ≈ 480, psyllium ≈ 260. This determines volume per bag.

3

Packing efficiency

Bags don't stack perfectly. We apply a 85–88% packing efficiency factor standard in the export industry.

4

Limiting factor

The lower of weight capacity vs volume capacity is your real limit. The calculator shows which applies to your product.
