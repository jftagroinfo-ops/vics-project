Trade FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before placing your first order.

What are your standard Payment Terms?

We work on 30% Advance + 70% against Scan BL. For established clients, we accept 100% Irrevocable LC at sight from top-tier banks. First-time buyers are encouraged to use LC for added security on both sides.

What is your Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)?

Generally one 20ft FCL = 24–26 MT depending on product density. For high-value spices or pharma-grade products, we can arrange LCL shipments. Sample orders are welcome — tick the sample checkbox in the form above.

Do you provide SGS / Third-Party Inspection?

Yes. We actively encourage inspections by SGS, Bureau Veritas, or Cotecna at loading. The inspection covers quantity, quality grading, container condition, and lab sampling. Cost is typically USD 250–450 per container.

Can you do Private Labeling / OEM Packaging?

Yes. Full private-label packaging on PP woven, non-woven, or jute bags in any size from 1kg to 50kg. In-house design team. MOQ for custom print: 1 FCL. Lead time for new artwork: 2–3 weeks.

What export documents do you provide?

Standard: Commercial Invoice, Packing List, Bill of Lading, Certificate of Origin (APEDA), Phytosanitary Certificate, Fumigation Certificate, and Certificate of Analysis. Additional on request: SGS cert, health cert, embassy-legalised CoO, halal cert.

How long from order to shipment?

In-stock commodities: 14–21 days from advance payment to container loading. Custom-packed or value-added products: 21–28 days. Sea transit from Mundra to African/Gulf ports: 8–22 days depending on destination.
