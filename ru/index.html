How Every Order
Gets Executed

01

Inquiry & Proforma

Client specs analysis and competitive proforma invoice within 24 hours — including CIF/FOB breakdowns.

02

Processing & Milling

In-house milling, grading, Sortex optical cleaning, and moisture reduction to export standards.

03

Quality Certification

SGS/Intertek pre-shipment inspection with full lab report. Phytosanitary and fumigation certificates issued.

04

Port & Vessel Loading

Container stuffing at APMC Vashi, port drayage to JNPT/Mundra, vessel loading with surveyor oversight.

05

Docs & Final Dispatch

Original BL release, SWIFT payment, certificate of origin, packing list, commercial invoice — complete set.
