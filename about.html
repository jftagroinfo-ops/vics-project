The 4-Stage
Quality Protocol

Our "No-Rejection" policy is built on a rigorous testing framework. Every container undergoes four levels of scrutiny before sealing.

01

Lab Analysis

Raw material tested for moisture (12-14%), admixture, and pesticide residue levels (MRL).

02

Sortex Purification

Advanced Buhler Sortex machines remove discolored grains, glass, and impurities.

03

SGS / Intertek

Third-party surveyors inspect cargo during loading for weight, quantity, and quality.

04

Fumigation

Mandatory Methyl Bromide / Phosphine fumigation to ensure pest-free arrival.
