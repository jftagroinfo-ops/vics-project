Who We Are

Rooted in India,
Reaching the World

Established in 1980, JFT Agro Overseas began as a humble domestic trading house in the grain markets of Mumbai. Today, we stand as a Government Recognized Star Export House, bridging the gap between India's fertile fields and the global food supply chain.

"We don't just export commodities; we export trust. Every grain that leaves our factory carries the reputation of Indian Agriculture."

Unlike simple traders, we are Processors & Manufacturers. With our own milling units and primary warehousing at APMC Vashi, Mumbai — just 12 km from JNPT port — we control the entire supply chain from procurement at the Mandi (farm market) to the final loading onto the vessel.

The 4-Stage
Quality Protocol

Our "No-Rejection" policy is built on a rigorous testing framework. Every container undergoes four levels of scrutiny before sealing — no exceptions.

01

Lab Analysis

Raw material tested for moisture (12–14%), admixture levels, pesticide residue (MRL) and aflatoxin before acceptance into our processing line.

02

Sortex Purification

Advanced Bühler Sortex Z+ optical machines remove discoloured grains, stones, glass fragments, and all foreign material to buyer-specified tolerances.

03

SGS / Intertek

Independent third-party surveyors from SGS or Intertek inspect cargo during loading — verifying weight, quality, and container stuffing standards.

04

Reference Retention

A sealed reference sample from every container is stored in our NABL lab for 18 months post-shipment — irrefutable evidence in any claim scenario.

Importing from India:
The Buyer's Guide

We simplify the complex world of agro-commodity imports for our global partners — from documentation to logistics choice.

1. Documentation

Understand the essential export documents: Bill of Lading, Phytosanitary Certificate, Certificate of Origin (COO), Health Certificate, and Fumigation Certificate required for your specific import country and port.

2. Quality Grading

Learn how to differentiate between 1121 Sella, Steam, and Raw Rice grades — or the Curcumin levels in Turmeric — to ensure you get the right quality and value for your specific market and end-use.

3. Logistics Choice

Compare FOB vs CIF pricing terms. We help you select the best shipping lines (Maersk, MSC, CMA CGM) to optimize transit time, container safety, and overall landed cost for your destination port.
