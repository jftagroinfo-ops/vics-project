Who We Are

Government Recognized Star Export House

Established in 1980, JFT Agro Overseas has evolved from a domestic grain trader to a global export powerhouse. Headquartered in Mumbai, the commercial capital of India, we specialize in the procurement, processing, and export of premium quality agricultural commodities.

We are not just traders; we are manufacturers. With our own state-of-the-art rice milling units in Haryana and spices processing facilities in Kerala, we control the entire supply chain—from farm to fork. This allows us to guarantee quality, consistency, and competitive pricing that few others can match.

Our Mission: To position India as the world's "Food Basket" by delivering pure, unadulterated, and high-quality agro-products to every corner of the globe.

Quality First

We believe quality is not an act, but a habit. Our in-house labs test every batch for moisture, purity, and pesticide residue before shipment.

Integrity

Our word is our bond. We have built 40+ years of relationships on transparency, timely delivery, and honoring our contracts.

Global Reach

With a robust logistics network, we successfully export to 25+ countries across Africa, the Middle East, Europe, and Southeast Asia.

Farmer Connect Program

At the heart of JFT Agro is the Indian Farmer. We have established direct contract farming agreements with over 5,000 farmers across India. By eliminating middlemen, we ensure that:

  • Farmers receive fair, above-market prices for their produce.
  • We get access to the freshest, highest-quality raw materials.
  • Sustainable farming practices are encouraged and rewarded.
