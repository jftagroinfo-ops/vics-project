Compliance

Asia Import Regulations

Halal Certification

Required for Indonesia, Malaysia, and Brunei. We work with HALAL India-certified inspectors to provide halal certificates for rice, spices, and processed products.

Phytosanitary (NPPO)

All cereals and spices require a Phytosanitary Certificate from India's National Plant Protection Organization. Issued automatically with every export shipment.

ASEAN FTA Benefits

India–ASEAN FTA provides preferential duty rates for most agricultural products. We advise on HS codes to ensure maximum tariff benefit for your imports.

Pesticide MRL Compliance

Vietnam, Singapore, and Japan enforce strict MRL limits aligned with Codex. We provide full pesticide residue lab reports from SGS/Bureau Veritas before shipment.
