How It Works

Track your shipment in 4 steps

Find your reference

Your container number or BL is in your JFT Agro Booking Confirmation email or Proforma Invoice.

Enter it above

Type your container number (e.g. MSCU1234567) or BL number in the search box and click Track.

Carrier identified

We instantly detect your shipping line from the container prefix and show the carrier's direct tracking link.

See live position

Click the tracking link to see your vessel's live position, port of loading, ETA at destination, and customs status.
