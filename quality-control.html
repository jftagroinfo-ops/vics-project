Zero-Compromise Quality Control

From pre-shipment SGS inspections to strict EU MRL compliance, JFT Agro guarantees that every container shipped matches your exact contractual specifications.

Laboratory Testing

Every batch of spices and rice is tested for Aflatoxin, moisture levels, ochratoxin, and pesticide residues (MRL) in NABL-accredited labs before packaging.

Fumigation & Phytosanitary

We execute strict fumigation protocols (Methyl Bromide or Aluminum Phosphide) as per the importing country's regulations to guarantee pest-free cargo.

Third-Party Inspection

We invite global inspection agencies like SGS, Intertek, or Bureau Veritas for weight, quality, and stuffing certification at the loading port.

EU & US Market Compliant

Exporting to highly regulated markets requires precision. JFT Agro specializes in supplying pesticide-free and IPM (Integrated Pest Management) commodities that easily pass the strict European Union MRL (Maximum Residue Limit) standards.

Our 4-Stage Traceability

  • Farm & Mandi Procurement

    We source directly from trusted farmer networks and major APMC mandis, ensuring crop freshness and authentic origin.

  • Sortex & Processing

    Goods are passed through Buhler color sortex machines to remove 100% of foreign matter, discolored grains, and impurities.

  • Hygienic Packaging

    Packed in food-grade PP, Jute, or BOPP bags using automated bagging lines to prevent human contact and contamination.

  • Container Stuffing

    Containers are inspected for cleanliness, lined with craft paper/silica gel if required, and sealed with a custom customs seal.
