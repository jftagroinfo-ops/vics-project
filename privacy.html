JFT Agro Overseas LLP ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our export services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us through our Request for Quote (RFQ) forms, newsletter signups, and contact requests. This may include:

  • Name and Company Name
  • Contact Information (Email, Phone, WhatsApp)
  • Import/Export Requirements and Port of Destination
  • Business VAT/Tax IDs (if provided for documentation)

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is primarily used to facilitate international trade operations. We use it to:

  • Prepare custom Proforma Invoices (PI) and quotes.
  • Manage shipping logistics and documentation.
  • Send trade insights and product price updates (if opted-in).
  • Comply with DGFT and Customs regulations in India.

3. Data Protection (GDPR Compliance)

For our partners in the European Union, we adhere to GDPR standards. We do not sell your business data to third-party marketing firms. Your information is only shared with authorized logistics partners (shipping lines/CHA) necessary to complete your export orders.

4. Cookies

We use cookies to analyze site traffic and improve our user experience. You can manage your cookie preferences via the banner on our homepage.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding your data, please contact our Data Compliance Officer at jftagro.info@gmail.com.
