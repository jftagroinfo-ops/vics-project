Compliance

UAE Import Regulations

Key compliance requirements we manage on your behalf for smooth UAE customs clearance:

ESMA / Halal Labeling

UAE requires Arabic labeling and ESMA-conforming nutritional panels. We support private-label packaging with Arabic text for retail-ready products.

MOCCAE Phytosanitary

Phytosanitary Certificate from India's National Plant Protection Organization (NPPO) is required. We arrange this with every shipment at no extra cost.

FIRS Registration (Dubai)

Food products entering Dubai Emirate must be registered with Dubai Municipality FIRS. We have existing registrations for most product categories.

Import Duty

India–UAE CEPA (signed 2022) provides preferential duty rates. Rice: 0% duty. Spices: 5% standard rate. Sugar: subject to quota. Always verify current tariffs with UAE customs.

Certificate of Origin

COO issued by APEDA or FIEO, legalized by UAE Embassy New Delhi. We process all documentation within 3 working days of LC opening.

Pesticide Residue Limits

UAE follows Codex Alimentarius MRLs. We conduct pre-shipment pesticide residue testing through SGS/Intertek — results shared before loading.
