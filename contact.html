Help Center

Trade FAQ

Common questions about ordering, shipping, and payments.

What are your standard Payment Terms?
We typically work on 30% Advance + 70% against Scan BL (Bill of Lading). For established clients, we accept 100% Irrevocable LC at sight from top-tier banks.
What is your Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)?
Our MOQ is generally one 20ft Full Container Load (FCL), which is approx 24-26 Metric Tons depending on the product and packing. We also offer small samples — please tick the sample checkbox in the form above.
Do you provide SGS Inspection?
Yes, we encourage third-party inspections (SGS, Bureau Veritas, Cotecna) at the time of loading to ensure quality and quantity transparency.
Can you do Private Labeling (OEM)?
Absolutely. We have in-house design capabilities to print your brand on PP bags, Non-Woven bags, or Jute bags as per your artwork specifications.
How do I calculate the estimated CIF price to my port?
Use our free CIF/FOB Quote Calculator — enter your product, quantity, and destination port to get an instant estimate before submitting a formal inquiry.
