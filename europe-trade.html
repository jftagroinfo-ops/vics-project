EU & UK Regulatory Standards
We Fully Meet

Europe sets the world's highest food safety standards. Here's our compliance framework — built for UK and EU importers:

Pesticide MRL Testing

We conduct Codex-aligned pesticide residue testing before every shipment. EU and UK enforce some of the world's strictest MRL limits — we test for all regulated substances through accredited labs (SGS, Eurofins, Intertek).

ISO 9001:2015 & HACCP

Our processing and export operations are certified under ISO 9001:2015 Quality Management System and HACCP (Hazard Analysis and Critical Control Points) — essential for European retailer and supermarket supply chains.

EU Organic (NPOP)

Organic-certified options available for select products (turmeric, sesame, coriander) under India's NPOP system — mutually recognized by the EU. Advance notice required for organic orders.

Basmati GI & UK Regulations

All Basmati rice exports comply with the UK's post-Brexit Basmati import standards including variety verification and DNA testing. We provide full traceability from field to container.
